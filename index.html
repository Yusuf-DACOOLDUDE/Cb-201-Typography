Trees and Their Amazingness



What Are Trees

A tree is a tall, perennial plant with a woody stem (trunk), branches, and leaves, designed to live for many years. Trees are defined by their woody vascular structure, which allows them to grow over 5 meters tall at maturity, distinguishing them from shrubs.

How are trees important

Trees are essential for life, providing oxygen, filtering pollutants, and combating climate change by storing carbon. They regulate the environment by cooling air, preventing soil erosion, and managing water runoff. Trees also offer habitats for wildlife, produce food, and improve mental and physical health in urban areas
